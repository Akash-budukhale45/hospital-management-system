Why Digital Hospitality?

The hospitality industry has rapidly transformed with the adoption of digital information systems. Hotels now rely on structured digital platforms to present transparent ratings, reviews, and feedback that directly influence user trust and decision-making.

Transparency & Trust

Digital ratings and verified reviews ensure transparency, helping users trust hospitality services before making decisions.

Informed Decision-Making

Sorting mechanisms such as most relevant, recent, and lowest-rated reviews allow users to interpret information from multiple perspectives.

Research & Analysis

Digital hospitality systems enable comparative analysis of user feedback, supporting academic research and service improvement.

How This System Works

A step-by-step overview of how digital information flows within the hospitality platform to support user decision-making.

1

Explore Hotels

Users browse hotel services presented as part of the hospitality ecosystem with clear ratings and descriptions.

2

Read Reviews & Ratings

Verified users share ratings and feedback that reflect real hospitality experiences.

3

Compare Using Sorting

Reviews are sorted by relevance, recency, and lowest rating to analyze information presentation effects.

4

Informed Decisions

Users interpret digital feedback transparently and make informed hospitality choices.

Research Insight & Conclusion

This digital hospitality information system demonstrates how user-generated ratings, reviews, and sorting mechanisms influence information transparency, trust, and decision-making on hotel platforms.

Impact of Sorting Mechanisms

Different review sorting methods present the same data in varied ways, subtly influencing user perception and evaluation of hotel services.

User Trust & Transparency

Verified reviews and visible sentiment classification enhance platform credibility and reduce information bias.

Hospitality Decision-Making

Structured digital feedback empowers users to make informed hospitality choices based on collective experience rather than isolated opinions.

Research Objective

This system studies how digital feedback mechanisms—such as rating aggregation, review sorting, and sentiment classification—impact decision-making on hospitality platforms.
